Accessibility Checklist

Readable fonts

Using sans serif fonts such as Arial, Calibri or Tahoma.
Font is size 12-14 or above.
Character spacing is set to expand 0.5, around 25% of letter width.
Text is not underlined or italicised, bold should be used instead in situations where appropriate.

Headings and Structure

Headings are at least 20% larger than normal text.
Text is broken into smaller chunks wherever appropriate.
Bullet points are ended in full stops.
Line spacing is 1.15.
Include a key for variables wherever there is space and it is appropriate.

Colour

Background with content over it is single colour and not patterned, borders without content over the top are fine.
Sufficient contrast between background and text.
Green text and red text are not used together.
Cream or soft pastel background can be preferable to white.

Layout

Text is left aligned.
Text is not in multiple columns.
Lines are no longer than 60-70 characters.
Related content is grouped where appropriate.
Text is broken up by headings where appropriate.
